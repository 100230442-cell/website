BURNLEY ARTS FESTIVAL

ABOUT

Welcome to the Burnley Art Festival, an annual celebration dedicated to uplifting and supporting the incredible creative talent within our local community. Our festival transforms Burnley into a vibrant canvas, featuring diverse exhibits, engaging live events, and delicious food stalls. We provide a dynamic platform for local artists to showcase their work, connect with art enthusiasts, and thrive. Join us in celebrating the rich artistic spirit of Burnley and discover your next favourite local creative.

OUR LOCATION

The festival is tri-monthly at the picturesque Inn on the Wharf. Plan your visit using the interactive map below.
