BURNLEY ARTS FESTIVAL

CATERING

La Délicatesse

Transport yourself to the streets of Montmartre with La Délicatesse, our resident festival food truck bringing a touch of Parisian elegance to Burnley. Open throughout the day, this charming mobile café specializes in the art of the perfect break: serving steaming cups of rich, Parisian-style coffee alongside a rotating selection of freshly baked pastries. From the shatteringly crisp layers of their signature golden croissants to decadent, chocolate-filled pains au chocolat, every bite is crafted to offer an authentic taste of France. Whether you need a morning pick-me-up or a sophisticated afternoon treat while you browse the galleries, La Délicatesse provides a warm, fragrant sanctuary for foodies and art lovers alike.

Jaipur

Experience the vibrant soul of Rajasthan at the Jaipur festival stall, where Burnley’s beloved local Indian restaurant brings the bold, aromatic flavors of the Pink City to life. Operating all day, Jaipur offers a sensory journey through traditional North Indian street food, specializing in fragrant slow-cooked biryanis and their signature handmade samosas, famous for their perfectly spiced potato and pea filling. Each dish is a celebration of heritage, utilizing authentic spice blends that have been passed down through generations to create a balance of warmth and flavor. Whether you are looking for a hearty midday meal or a spicy snack to fuel your walk through the exhibitions, Jaipur provides a colorful and welcoming taste of India right in the heart of the festival.

HOME

Savor the comforting flavors of the East at Home, a celebrated local favorite bringing authentic Chinese soul food to the heart of the festival. Known for their "tastes like home" philosophy, this stall serves up a vibrant menu of classic street-side delicacies throughout the day. From steaming baskets of handmade dumplings to wok-fired specialty noodles tossed with fresh, local ingredients, every dish captures the perfect balance of sweet, savory, and spice. Whether you are craving the crunch of their famous golden spring rolls or a warming bowl of savory beef brisket, Home offers a nourishing and delicious retreat for festival-goers looking for a genuine taste of Chinese culinary tradition.

Big B's

Get ready to sink your teeth into a festival favorite at Big B’s, the high-energy burger joint bringing a sizzle to the local food lineup. Known for their legendary technique, Big B's serves up heavyweight gourmet burgers made from locally sourced beef and tucked into toasted, buttery brioche buns. Whether you're diving into a classic double cheeseburger dripping with their top-secret B-Sauce or grabbing a side of their famous loaded rosemary fries, every order is built for maximum flavor and indulgence. It’s the ultimate pit-stop for festival-goers looking for a bold, hearty meal that packs a punch and keeps you fueled for a full day of art and exploration.

Bella's Bakes

Indulge your sweet tooth at Bella’s Bakes, the festival’s premier dessert destination known for turning classic bakes into edible art. This locally-owned stall is a sugar-lover’s paradise, serving up a rotating display of artisan cupcakes, gooey deep-dish brownies, and their famous giant stuffed cookies throughout the day. Every treat is baked from scratch using premium ingredients, ensuring that each bite is as fresh as it is beautiful. Whether you’re stopping by for a mid-afternoon sugar boost or picking up a decadent treat to enjoy during a live performance, Bella’s Bakes provides a whimsical and delicious touch of sweetness to your festival experience.
