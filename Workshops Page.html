BURNLEY ARTS FESTIVAL

WORKSHOPS

Pottery Making

Our hourly pottery sessions run daily from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, offering a fast-paced but creative escape into the world of clay. Whether you’re a complete beginner or a seasoned pro, these one-hour slots are designed to get your hands dirty and your imagination firing.

Basket Weaving

Our Traditional Willow Weaving sessions offer a peaceful escape into a craft that has remained largely unchanged for thousands of years. These sessions run from 9:30 AM to 5:30 PM, starting every hour on the half-hour.

Paper Statue Contest

Our Paper Statue "Sculpt-Off" is a high-energy, one-time-only event that challenges your speed and structural creativity. Unlike our daily workshops, this is a competitive battle of wits and folds where participants have 90 minutes to transform flat sheets of cardstock into a 3D masterpiece.
